Parameters
sceneThe scene.
newPositionsThe buffer into which to store the "new" positions of the surfels from their most recent merges.
oldPositionsThe buffer into which to store the "old" position of the surfels from their most recent merges.
correspondencesThe buffer into which to store the "new" and "old" positions of the surfels for the purpose of rendering line segments between them.
